Nim Dragons

Click for Instructions

Nim Dragons is a two-player game, wherein players take turns removing bubbles from the game board. Here are the rules:
  1. Bubbles can only be removed within a single row, so you can't make vertical or diagonal moves in your turn.
  2. You can remove one bubble from the row, or choose to remove the entire row in your turn.
  3. The last player able to move wins the game!
